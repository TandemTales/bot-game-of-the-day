Comet Carom

Drag from the launch comet to aim and set power, then release to flick. Clear every rogue satellite before you run out of shots. Desktop backup controls: Left/Right aim, Up/Down power, Space fire, P pause, R restart.

How To Play

  • Sink rogue satellites into the glowing sun or knock them through the four warp docks on the rim.
  • Your shot only earns a vortex bonus after it has touched at least one target, so reckless center dives waste tempo.
  • If your comet survives the shot, it scores a ring bonus where it settles before the next launch.

Keyboard Assist

Arrow keys aim and trim power even while the pointer is idle. Space launches. Enter starts from the title or replays from game over.
