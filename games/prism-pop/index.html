Prism Pop

Aim with mouse or touch, launch with click or tap, swap with Shift or the swap button, and clear color clusters before the lantern ceiling crushes the stage. Built as an original art-and-audio riff on 16-bit bubble shooters.

How To Play

  • Match 3 or more lantern orbs of the same color to pop them.
  • Anything disconnected from the ceiling falls for bonus points.
  • The danger line rises after every few shots, so clean combos matter.

Controls

Desktop: move the mouse to aim, click or Space to fire, Shift to swap the held orb, P to pause.

Touch Controls

Original code, art, and audio. Responsive on desktop and mobile browsers.
