Patch Parade

Move with arrow keys or A/D, jump with up/W/space, throw patch with X/K/click. Collect runaway buttons, repair torn cloth, and swat silk moths before the parade unravels.

How It Works

Buttons keep the parade alive. Frayed platforms can tear open at any time. Your thrown patch does two jobs: it repairs the first torn patch of cloth it reaches, or stuns a moth if it gets there first. Each cleared wave speeds up the chaos and adds more score multiplier pressure.
