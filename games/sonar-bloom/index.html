Sonar Bloom

Pilot a bioluminescent sub through the crushing dark. Steer with your finger or mouse, your lance auto-fires, and the abyss blooms with anglerfish, jelly swarms, and rising leviathans. Grab glowing cells to grow your weapon and unleash a Sonar Pulse when the swarm closes in.

How To Play

  • Drag to move your sub — your lance fires on its own.
  • Destroy creatures, dodge their glowing spit.
  • Collect ◈ cells to level up your weapon.
  • Grab ◇ shields and ◎ pulses.
  • Survive the waves; every 5th, a Leviathan rises.
  • Chain kills fast to build a score multiplier.

Controls

Desktop: move mouse to steer. Space / B fires a Sonar Pulse. P pauses, M mutes.

Mobile: drag the stage to steer; tap Pulse below.
