Astral Sling

Orbit a star, then tap or press Space to sling free at exactly the right moment. Curve through gravity, get caught by the next star, and chain hops across an endless galaxy. One button — pure orbital timing.

How To Play

  • Your craft circles the glowing star it is locked to.
  • Release when the dashed arc points at the next star.
  • Land in a star's pull to lock on and score a hop.
  • Sweep up stardust mid-flight for bonus points.
  • Chain quick hops to build a score multiplier.
  • Fly into empty space and you're lost — game over.

Controls

Desktop: click, Space, or Enter to sling. P pauses. M mutes.

Mobile: tap the stage or the Sling button below.
