How To Play

  • Roll, then close any open combination of shutters that adds up to the dice total.
  • Once 7, 8, and 9 are sealed, you may toggle down to one die for classic endgame control.
  • If a roll has no legal combination, the round floods and you lose one lantern.
  • Shut all nine numbers for a full-clear bonus and an extra lantern.

Controls

Desktop: number keys 1-9 select shutters, R or Space rolls, Enter seals, Backspace clears, D changes dice count, M mutes.
